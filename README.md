## What is Random Spaces?

__Random Spaces__ is a free, open learning resource for students and researchers who interested in applying Python to solve complex problems, to manage and tranform data, and to produce publication-quality figures. The tutorials here bridge the gap beetween mathematics, chemistry and computational tools.

All materials are shared openly on my repositories [![Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/github-repositories-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=black&logoSize=auto&label=github&labelColor=hex&color=black)](https://github.com/dindagustiayu?tab=repositories)
